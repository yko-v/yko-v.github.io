Основные характеристики

Provide an In-Orbit Demonstration (IOD) and In-Orbit Verification (IOV) service in space, as part of a Horizon 2020 funded activity to strengthen the competitiveness of the EU space industry. The European Commission has baselined a number of novel technology experiments for demonstration in low earth orbit. ISISpace is pleased to be selected by ESA to implement this activity by accommodating these experiments onboard two 6-Unit CubeSat platforms and to provide the experiment operations after launch.
