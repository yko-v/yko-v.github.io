Семейство Cute

Описание

Communication, sensing and deployment mechanism missions. CW-transmitter and FM-transmitter. FM-telemetry by two different protocol (Ax.25 and TITech original. Broadcasting service of CW-telemetry data and FM-telemetry data on WWW. Deploy the solar battery paddle by command uplink.
