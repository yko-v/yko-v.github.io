Семейство khors-1

Описание

Earth Observation (EO) and Automatic Identification Systems (AIS) applications. This project marks a vital milestone in ANGKASA-X’s endeavor to deploy a constellation of Low Earth Orbit (LEO) satellites in the near future, which will be developed and assembled in Malaysia, solidifying the nation’s role as a significant player in the global space industry and the space tech ecosystem.
