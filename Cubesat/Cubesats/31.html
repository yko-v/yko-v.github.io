Семейство ASELSAT

Описание

GSD of at least 30 meter payload and X Band downlink system is designed and developed. House the X Band transmitter and patch antenna developed by ASELSAN Co, GomSpace NanoCam, SU 3 axis ADCS, n-ART OBCommS by ErTek among others. The 3U structure called n-ART Structure is specifically developed for ASELSAT by Gumush Space Ltd.
