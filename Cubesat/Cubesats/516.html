Семейство Pico

Описание

The operational satellites would be 1/3U, or a third of the size of a 10 x 10 x 10-centimeter cubesat. Using very high frequency (VHF) band spectrum, Apogeo plans to use its network of tiny satellites to connect low-power, battery-powered devices outside of terrestrial network coverage. A network of 20 satellites would be enough to retrieve data from devices anywhere on the globe with a maximum 30-minute wait time, according to Apogeo, and the full constellation would enable continuous coverage.
