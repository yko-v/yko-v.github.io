Семейство Tevel

Описание

A mission with 8 identical CubeSats. High-school students’ educational satellite, high schools will only receive the telemetry beacons for educational research activities. Amateur radio transponder. The FM amateur radio transponder will be the only payload. They can be commanded to operate as U/V FM transponders.
