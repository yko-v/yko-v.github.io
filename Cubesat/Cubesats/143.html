Семейство CubeBel

Описание

1) To test active magnetic attitude control algorithms; 2) To collect scientific data from radiation spectrometer; 3) To locate position using GPS receiver; 4) To take pictures of Earth from Low Earth Orbit; 5) To collect scientific data from IR sensor. Navigator, a radiation spectrometer, an infrared sensor, and radiation-resistant elements.
