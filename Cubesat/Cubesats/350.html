Семейство LabSat

Описание

Packed with scientific payloads from Wrocław University of Science and Technology and three other Polish academic institutions. Researching the behavior of cancer cells and T lymphocytes, as well as the growth of fungi. Miniature laboratory based on MEMS (Micro Electro-Mechanical Systems) technology, which combines micromechanical and microelectronic elements. The CubeSat nanosatellite, which the company will provide, will be equipped with miniaturized lab-on-chip instruments
