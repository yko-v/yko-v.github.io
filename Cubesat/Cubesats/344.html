Семейство LEDSAT

Описание

Equipped with LEDs (Light Emitting Diodes) and retroreflectors for optical tracking with ground-based telescopes and laser ranging observatories. Testing orbit and attitude determination by means of ground based optical observation and testing LED based optical communication. LEDSAT will equip a bespoke digipeater system with both real tile and “store & forward” capabilities for radio-amateur use; the radio-amateur user can define the delay in the retransmission of the message in a range of 0sec to 24hours.
