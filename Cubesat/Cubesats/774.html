Семейство Yarilo

Описание

Record solar activity in the x-ray range. Possible to obtain an operational forecast of the state of the interplanetary medium. The grouping will include two microsatellites, which will be scattered in orbit using the solar sail. Communication with the devices will be maintained from the Mission Control Center of MSTU. LPI provides a payload (X-ray spectrophotometer) because the laboratory cannot afford to buy it.
