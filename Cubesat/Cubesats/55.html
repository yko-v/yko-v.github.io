Семейство Astrocast

Описание

Low data M2M and IoT services. The goal of the mission is to demonstrate all the satellite functionalities and multi satellite operations. Radio Amateur mission: Data echo. Pseudo Random Binary Sequence ranging beacon (that will be validated with laser ranging system as corner cube reflector are present on the satellite). Beacon with Telemetry data (satellite housekeeping and pre-defined and scheduled). Telemetry with sensors pictures.
